What is ApresMafia?

Are you looking for your next skiing adventure, apres-ski spot, or tips to help improve your skiing abilities? ApresMafia is a compilation of all information about the ski industry. Find your next destination with details on how to take full advantage of the location and the inside scoop on cool spots that you otherwise may not know about. Find places to go after your ski day ends, from great apres-ski spots to fun stuff to do with the family around each ski resort. If you are looking to improve your skiing abilities or are trying to look less like a jerry on the mountain, you can find tips and tricks to keep in mind for your next alpine endeavor.
