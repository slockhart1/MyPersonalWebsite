About Me

Hi, my name is Sam and welcome to my own personal website! A few things to know about me are that I was born and raised in a small town outside Boston called Topsfield and I like to play soccer, ultimate disc, and ski. I have one older brother who is two years older than me and just graduted from Clarkson University. Also, I have a cat name Peyton and don't worry she actually really enjoys being held like this.

I chose to come to Babson College because it is an entreprenuership school and my brain is constantly coming up with new ideas which I may want to pursue in the future. I also wanted to go somewhere that is a driving distance from my house and had small class sizes. For all of these reasons and many more, Babson seemed to be in the sweet spot for everything I was looking for in a school.

My Hobbies

My favortie hobby is skiing and I have been participating in this activity ever since I was <3 years old. Over the past 18 years, I have gained an extensive amount of knowledge and expirience on and off the slopes. The type of skiing I like to do is skiing mostly off-piste, though as a New England resident I rarely get the chance. One of my favorite videos that shows off-piste skiing is Just one of those Days by Candide Thovex. I love this video because he shows what peak skiing ability looks like.
