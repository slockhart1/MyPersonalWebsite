Welcome to ApresMafia!

Are you looking for your next skiing adventure, apres-ski spot, or tips to help improve your skiing abilities? Well, I may not be able to provide you with that at the moment but I plan on being able to offer all that as I journey from resort to resort. This is my personal website where I compile information about the ski industry. It may not be much now, but I plan to review any resorts I go to with included information on my favorite trails, chairs, and apres-ski spots.

My goal with this website is to provide myself with a log of information the destinations I've been to. This website is mostly for myself but with the added benefit of possibly helping other skiers. I will be sharing all my knowledge here and I have made it my mission to display it in a good looking format.
